NAVIGATION TABS & CONTENT

1. HOME
Purpose:
Welcome visitors and introduce Gatimu School.

Sections:
• Hero Banner
  - Title: "Welcome to Gatimu School"
  - Motto or inspirational statement
  - Background image of the school

• Image Carousel
  - School compound photos
  - Learning environment
  - Activities
  - Facilities

• Short Welcome Message
  Tone:
   - Warm
   - Respectful
   - Focused on education, discipline & growth

• Key Highlights
  - Quality teaching
  - Student development
  - Discipline & values
  - Community impact

• Quick Buttons
  - Learn More
  - Contact Us
  - (Portal Coming Soon)

------------------------------------
2. ABOUT
Purpose:
Share information about Gatimu School.

Sections:
• School Overview
  Include:
  - Type of school
  - Location
  - Brief background

• Vision Statement
• Mission Statement
• School Values
  Examples:
  - Discipline
  - Excellence
  - Respect
  - Integrity
  - Hard Work

• Message from the Administration
  (Short friendly statement)

• Why Choose Gatimu School?
  - Strong discipline
  - Supportive environment
  - Strong academics
  - Character building

------------------------------------
3. GALLERY
Purpose:
Showcase school life visually.

Content:
• Photo collection including:
  - School buildings
  - Classrooms
  - Learning in action
  - Co-curricular activities
  - School events

Notes:
• Use clear, appropriate photos
• Avoid exposing sensitive personal details

------------------------------------
4. CONTACT
Purpose:
Allow parents, students & visitors to reach the school.

Sections:
• Contact Information
  - Phone Number
  - Email Address
  - Location / Address

• Office Hours
  Example:
  Monday–Friday: 8:00am – 5:00pm

• Optional:
  - Simple enquiry message (future contact form)
  - Map embed later

------------------------------------

Future digital student information system.
